Our Mission: Make Cyber Education Accessible.

This doctoral research study at The University of Alabama explores how experiential, CTF-based learning can increase student engagement and understanding in high school cybersecurity classrooms across Alabama.

By connecting real-world cyber skills to the state's K–12 Computer Science Standards, we're building a bridge between classroom theory and hands-on technical practice.

  • Encourage critical thinking and problem-solving through real challenges
  • Support teachers with ready-to-use CTF activities and lesson guides
  • Inspire students to explore cybersecurity careers
  • Contribute to statewide improvements in CS education
  • All participation is voluntary, anonymous, and IRB-approved
