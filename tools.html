How to Sign Up

Getting Started with
CTFforCyberLearning

Follow these steps to create your CTFd account and get ready to tackle the Cyber Security challenges. Complete the pre-test first before beginning any challenges.

// sign-up steps

Follow These Steps

1

Go to the CTFd Website

Visit the CTFforCyberLearning challenge portal. You'll see a welcome page with a sign-up option.

ex4cslearning.ctfd.io →
2

Complete the Pre-Test First

Before beginning any challenges, complete the short pre-test. It only takes about 5 minutes and measures your starting knowledge. Return to this page after finishing.

Take the Pre-Test →
3

Click "Sign Up" on CTFd

On the top right of the CTFd page, click Sign Up. Enter your school email address, create a secure password, and re-enter it to confirm.

Use your school email if possible. Do not use your personal or family email address.
4

Join Your Team (if applicable)

If your researcher provides a team code or school group, enter that code during registration or from the Teams tab after signing up. If not, you can proceed as an individual student.

5

Verify and Log In

Check your email (including spam folder) for a confirmation message. Click the verification link, then log in and start exploring your challenges!

// important rules

Important Rules

  • Do not share flags or answers with other students
  • Do not change your username after signing up
  • If you forget your password, use the "Forgot Password" option on the login screen
  • Ask the researcher before making any profile changes
  • Your CTFd username should only identify your school and initials — never use your full name
