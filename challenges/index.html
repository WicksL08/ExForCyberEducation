// teacher responsibilities

Before the Study

  • Complete teacher consent form via DocuSign
  • Verify all student/parent consent forms are submitted
  • Ensure every student has device access and the CTFd link
  • Review the 3-day syllabus and standards mapping

During the Study

  • Facilitate mini-lessons — provide guidance, not answers
  • Ensure students complete the pre-test before challenges
  • Monitor participation and record observations
  • Review the Code of Conduct with students before starting

After the Study

  • Ensure students complete the post-test
  • Submit teacher reflections using the feedback form
  • Report any technical issues to the research team

Ethics & Conduct

  • All work must occur within the CTFd environment only
  • No unauthorized access or real-world system testing
  • Respect student privacy and your school's AUP
  • Review the Ethics Policy with students
