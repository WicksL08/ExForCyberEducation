# Student Code of Conduct & Agreement 

Welcome to **Ex4CSLearning**, a hands-on cyber security learning experience designed to make cyber concepts fun, practical, and ethical.  
Before participating in any activity or Capture the Flag (CTF) challenge, please read and agree to the following expectations.

---

##  Purpose of This Agreement

The goal of this document is to ensure all students understand how to:
- Participate **ethically** and responsibly in all cyber security activities.  
- Protect their own **privacy and digital safety**.  
- Respect others and maintain a positive learning environment.  
- Contribute to research in a **safe and anonymous** way.  

By following this agreement, students help create a trustworthy, inclusive, and professional learning space.

---

## Ethical Participation Guidelines

As a participant, I agree to:

✅ **Use my cyber security skills for good.**  
I will not use what I learn to harm people, systems, or data.  

✅ **Respect privacy.**  
I will never access, share, or post personal or school information without permission.  

✅ **Play fair.**  
I will complete challenges honestly without using outside tools or AI to find answers.  

✅ **Follow instructions.**  
I will only test or explore within the CTF environment provided by my teacher.  

✅ **Be respectful and supportive.**  
I will help others learn and maintain a positive environment for everyone.  

✅ **Ask for help.**  
If I encounter something I don’t understand or that feels wrong, I will notify my teacher or the project lead.

---

##  Data & Privacy Notice

This activity is part of an **IRB-approved research study** conducted through  
**The University of Alabama**.

- All participation is **voluntary**.  
- Student data is **anonymous and confidential**.  
- Teachers act as facilitators, not evaluators.  
- You may stop participating at any time without penalty.  
- Results are analyzed in aggregate to improve cyber security education.

No personal or identifying data is stored or published.

---

## Agreement

By participating in Ex4CSLearning activities, I agree to:

- Follow the ethical participation guidelines above.  
- Use my learning only for educational purposes.  
- Respect the privacy and integrity of all participants.  

> *“cyber security begins with integrity.”*

---

##  For Teachers & Guardians

Teachers and guardians may print or review this agreement with students before participation.  
This document ensures that everyone understands how to engage safely and ethically in cyber security learning.
