# Consent & Assent Forms 

Before participating in the **Ex4CSLearning** study, all participants must complete the appropriate electronic consent form(s).  
These DocuSign forms ensure that participation is voluntary, that data are handled ethically, and that all parties understand their rights and responsibilities.

---

## Important Information

- All forms must be completed **before** students take the [Pre-Test]({{ '/assessments/pre/' | relative_url }}) or begin any challenges.  
- Participation is voluntary. Students and teachers may withdraw at any time without penalty.  
- All data are stored securely under **The University of Alabama** research compliance policies.
