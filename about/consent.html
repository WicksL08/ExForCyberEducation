Voluntary Participation

Students and teachers may withdraw at any time without penalty or consequence.

Anonymous Data

All responses are anonymous. No individual is ever identified in any research output.

IRB Approved

This study has been reviewed and approved by The University of Alabama Institutional Review Board.

Secure Storage

All data are stored securely under UA research compliance policies.
