# About the Study ℹ️

**Ex4CSLearning** evaluates a Capture-the-Flag (CTF)-based experiential model for high-school cybersecurity education across Alabama.

- Teacher-led activities + student-friendly challenges  
- Short pre-/post-assessments to gauge learning and interest  
- Alignment with Alabama K-12 and NICE standards
