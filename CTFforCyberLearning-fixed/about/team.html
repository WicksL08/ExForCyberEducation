Research Team

The people behind CTFforCyberLearning — a collaborative effort at The University of Alabama to bring experiential cybersecurity into K–12 classrooms.

// project lead
LW

Lacey Wicks

Project Lead · Doctoral Candidate

Lacey leads the CTFforCyberLearning project and conducts research on how experiential learning methods — particularly Capture-the-Flag (CTF) activities — can improve student engagement and understanding of cybersecurity in K–12 settings. Her work combines educational theory, cybersecurity practice, and technology integration to design classroom-ready activities aligned with Alabama K–12 Computer Science Standards.
