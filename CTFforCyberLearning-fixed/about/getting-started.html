How to Sign Up for CTFd

Before solving challenges, you need to create a secure account on the CTFd platform. Follow the steps below carefully.

// before you begin — Make sure you have completed the Consent Forms and the Pre-Test before creating your CTFd account.
// sign-up steps
01

Go to the CTFd Website

Open your browser and visit the Ex4CSLearning challenge platform.

Open CTFd Platform →

You'll see a page that says "Welcome to Ex4CSLearning" with a sign-up option.

02

Complete the Pre-Test First

Before registering, complete the short pre-test. It only takes about 5 minutes and measures your learning progress.

Take the Pre-Test →
After completing the pre-test, return here and continue with the CTFd steps.
03

Click "Sign Up"

On the top right of the CTFd page, click Sign Up and fill in the fields:

  • Enter your school or temporary email address
  • Create a secure password you can remember
  • Re-enter the password to confirm
// note — Use your school email if possible. Do not use your personal or family email.
04

Join Your Team (if applicable)

If your teacher provides a team code or school group, enter that code during registration or from the Teams tab after signing up.

If not, you can proceed as an individual student.

05

Verify and Log In

After creating your account:

  • Check your email (including spam) for the confirmation message
  • Click the verification link
  • Log in and start exploring your challenges!
// important rules

Account Rules

  • Do not share flags or answers with other students
  • Do not change your username after sign up
  • Use the "Forgot Password" option if needed
  • Ask your teacher before making profile changes

Username Format

  • Your username should only identify your school and initials
  • Never use your full name in your username
  • Example: BHS_JD for Jefferson Davis at Brewer High
  • Ask your teacher if unsure

// need help?

If you have login issues or can't verify your email, contact the research team.
