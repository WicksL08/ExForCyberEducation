## Challenge Overview

| Day | Topic | Focus | Example Challenges |
|-----|--------|--------|--------------------|
| 1 | **OSINT** | Ethical online investigation & information verification | “Find the Bean,” “World Traveler,” “Vanished Motto” |
| 2 | **Cryptography** | Encoding, substitution, and decoding hidden messages | “Secret Scroll,” “Hidden Codebook” |
| 3 | **Password Security** | Hashing, salting, and password strength | “How Long Until It’s Gone,” “County Salt” |

> All challenges are hosted securely on the [CTFd Portal](https://ex4cslearning.ctfd.io).

##  Ethics & Conduct

Remind students that:
- All work must occur **within the CTFd environment** only.  
- No unauthorized access, scanning, or real-world probing.  
- Respect privacy and follow your school’s AUP.  
- Review the [Code of Conduct]({{ '/about/student-agreement/' | relative_url }}) before beginning.

---

## Teacher Responsibilities

- Verify all student and parent consent forms are completed.  
- Ensure every student has access to devices and the CTFd link.  
- Facilitate mini-lessons and provide guidance—not answers.  
- Record observations and feedback after each session.  
- Submit teacher reflections using the [Feedback Form]({{ '/about/feedback/' | relative_url }}).
